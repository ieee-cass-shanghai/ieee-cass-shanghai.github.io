Aims of the Forum

To promote our IEEE CASS vision in fostering interdisciplinary and cross-disciplinary talents and provide educational opportunities to our IEEE members and students in China, “IEEE CASS Forum on Interdisciplinary Research” will be held in hybrid format in Region 10, Shanghai, China on the 14th November 2021.

The forum covers from industry internet of things, to implantable/wearable brain-machine interface, to point-of-care diagnostics for fast virus screening. Shanghai being the epic-center of microelectronics industry and research, is one of the best locations to promote our IEEE CASS and encourage interdisciplinary collaboration in-between academic and industry. In this forum, we will help our IEEE members and students to have a better understanding in intelligent sensory systems, and how to establish multi-discipline research collaboration.

The venue for this forum is located in Shanghai Hongqiao Greenland Primus Hotel, adjacent to the Shanghai National Convention and Exhibition Center.
